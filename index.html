Solutions

Welcome! We are Brainway: a software house that specializes in developing software solutions and providing technological consulting to organizations.

End-to-End Solutions

Our development team is here from A to Z and is ready to empower your product in every stage of production. We will work with you on product definition, an in-depth analysis of your project’s needs to determine the technological means and tools necessary to get the work done. Once we have the proper foundation laid for your solution, we will then work through development and keep you informed each step of the way. After completion, we strive to provide 24/7 support and maintenance services to ensure the product is fully operational after deployment.

For Entrepreneurs & Start-Ups

We are here to accompany start-ups and entrepreneurs at every stage from conception to solution production, PoC, development, and initial product maintenance as it is exposed to the market. We pride ourselves on this adaptive partnership style because it gives you exactly what you need as a new business to get your product created without the hassle of working with multiple companies at each phase of creation.

Flexible Working Models

Having the flexibility to adjust the size of your team can be vital when it comes time to divert your budget from R&D to marketing or sales. With our adaptive approach, we will give you the ability to adjust your development team and even add part-time profiles like a CTO, Product Manager, or Chief Architect, as necessary. We will adjust the team’s size to fit your company’s current needs to offer you the expertise required to keep your project running; this will help you avoid unnecessary hiring and firing cycles and our experts will still remain available to you whenever you need it.

What’s your goal?

MVP

Rather than conducting extensive and expensive market research, you’ve chosen to start with an MVP? We can make something for you that just outlines the essentials to get it onto market and test and collect feedback on your product out in the real world. We are here to help with that!

Develop

You have a concrete outline of what you want from your tech-based solution? We are happy to get involved and build the exact product per your specifications, budget, and timeline in order to get you up and running as efficiently as possible.

Revamp

Have an existing system but you’re ready to step it up a notch? We can analyze your current offering and propose a new architecture to take your product to the next generation and ensure a smooth migration. Once the specific approach is chosen, we will lead you into a shiny new system that is ready for reimplementation in the market.

Launch

Do you have an idea for the next great tech product or service but don’t have the technical team to make it happen?Bring us on board and we will become your trusted technical partner and ensure that your idea is brought to life according to the highest industry standards.

Rescue

Are you in the middle of building your product but things just quite aren’t going your way? We can help you get everything back on track! Our team will analyze the situation and find the most effective and efficient solutions based on your particular needs so your product can be ready to launch as quickly as possible.
